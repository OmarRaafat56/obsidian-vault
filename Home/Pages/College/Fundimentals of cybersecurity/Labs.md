### information Gathering steps:


1. Unearthing intital information
2. Locate the network range
3. Ascertain active machines
4. Discover open ports/ access points
5. Detect operating systems
6. Uncover services on ports
7. Map the network

### Reconnaissance types

#### passive reconnaissance 

>involves acquiring information without directly interacting with the target.
>ex : searching public records or news releases

#### active reconnaissance

>involves interacting with the target directly by any means.
>ex : telephone calls to the help desk or technical department

### DNS (domain name system)


#### consists of:
- top-level domain (com, net, edu)
- second-level domain (Microsoft.com, Google.com)
- subdomains (research.microsoft.com)

To remove the dns resolver cache, type : #ipconfig /flushDNS
